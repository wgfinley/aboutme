Introduction

I'm a Front End developer who loves clean and modern design as well was WordPress. I operate my own company called WrenchTech Guy LLC where I focus on web design and hosting for small businesses and non-profits.

I've been in love with the internet since 1990 and created my very first website in 1997. My passion for the net is brought about by its ability to lower the barriers to entry for the small guy — the underdog.

I'm excited to start on the next phase of my development as a developer by being a part of the Thinkful Front End Web Development program. I look forward to further strengthening my skills working alongside the great community.

Where I'm From

I'm originally from far north Chicago suburbs. I few years ago I traded in the flatland to become a cheesehead in southeastern Wisconsin.

I love the small town atmosphere, the lack of traffic and the great feeling of community here. I look forward to helping the businesses here better their presence on the web.
